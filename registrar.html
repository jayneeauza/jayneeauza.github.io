ONLINE REQUEST
STEP 1

Download and complete Request Form

STEP 2

Scan and Request Form to the following:

CAMPUS EMAIL ADDRESS
Main Campus mercyjmlago@gmail.com
RNP Campus registrar_jhs@liceo.edu.ph
Paseo Campus ldcushiena2018@gmail.com

Include the following in your email:

  1. Student's ID (front and back)
  2. Other Accepted Government ID (front and back)
STEP 3

Wait for call or email from the Registrar for the assessment of fees for the documents and courier services. Requests will not be processed if student has incomplete credentials in the Registrar, and other liabilities in the University.

STEP 4

Pay online for documents requested at affiliated banks and payment centers of the University.

STEP 5

Send a copy of proof of payment. Wait for confirmation and status notification of request by the Registrar.
Turnaround time shall be 14- 20 business days.

For door to door delivery - Student shall receive document to the address indicated in the request form.

For pick up - Student shall bring valid identification card. If other person will pick up, bring ID of the owner of the document and authorization letter.

If other person will pick up, bring document owner's ID and authorization letter.
