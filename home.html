FDT EUROPE TOUR 2018

After the unparalleled success of their one-month Folklore tour in Europe, the Liceo Folkloric Dance Troupe will stage a HOMECOMING CONCERT on September 18 at the Rodelsa Hall.The Liceo FDT was the sole Asian Dance group invited to participate in seven international folklore festivals in France, Bulgaria, and Romania from June 29 to August 2 of this year. The first festival, the 35th Festival des Cultures du Monde was held in Voiron, France by the Arts & Popular Traditions Samorenc Voiron-Chartreuse, a French Folklore Dance Group whose Director is a founding member of the WORLD FOLKLORE UNION I.G.F.(IGF). In Voiron, the FDT joined groups from Madagascar, Macedonia, Paraguay and Israel-Palestine, and Canada in a week-long festival sharing cultures through daily performances and street animations.

HIV/AIDS Awareness and Substance AbuseEducation Seminar

October 3, 2018 - The NSTP class of the Medical Laboratory Science - Block D, with their instructor, Mr. Kimberly B. Alvarez, organized and hosted a seminar on HIV/AIDS Awareness and Substance Abuse Education held at AVR 3 in this university. It was associated with its point of engagement- what an individual can do to avoid the said issues.Mrs. Stella Ferrarez, a Psychologist and an employee of Philippine Mental Health Association was the guest speaker of the event. Mrs. Ferrarez emphasized on the spread of HIV/AIDS to the youngsters, its implications to one’s health and morality, and most specifically how this can be evaded. Her experience in the profession opened her mind to become an advocate.
