ADMISSION PROCEDURE
  • Step 1. Check Schedule for Entrance Exam
    Guidance and Testing Office | www.liceo.edu.ph | Facebook (Liceo de Cagayan University - Official FB)
  • Step 2. Proceed to University Cashier and pay Entrance Exam Fee.
  • Step 3. Proceed to Guidance and Testing Office and take the University Entrance Examination.
  • Step 4. Verify Entrance Exam Test Results through the testing officer, website and official Facebook account and follow instructions given
  • Step 5. For Entrance Exams Passers, register online at wwd.liceo.edu.ph/#/registration. Fill out all required fields. Click Submit and take note of Tracking Number. Print documents in legal size bond paper.
ENROLLMENT PROCEDURE
 Signature

Step 1. Proceed to Admissions Office and bring printed Admission Documents. Submit the original and two (2) photocopies of the following:

  • Form 138
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • Grade 12 Certificate of Completion with Learner Reference Number (LRN)
  • Birth Certificate
  • 4pcs. 2x2 picture
  • 2 long brown envelopes
  • 1 long folder with plastic jacket

(Note: Admissions Office shall provide temporary student number to open account)
Step 2. Proceed to Registrar’s Office and submit requirements.
Step 3. Proceed to the College Dean for interview.
Step 4. Proceed to University Cashier and pay down payment and other fees to open officail account.
Step 5. Proceed to the College Academic Coordinator for encoding and verification of subjects
Step 6. Proceed to the University Clinic for Medical Check-up.
Step 7. Proceed to Liceo Press for ID processing.
Step 8. Proceed to SASTRE for uniform purchase.
POST ENROLLMENT
  • Step 1. Attend Jumpstart: The Liceo U General Orientation
  • Step 2. Present orientation clearance signed by Student-Personnel Services and College for release of ID and student handbook.
