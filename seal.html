The University Seal

The School Seal represents the Institutional culture of the university. The different elements and the significance of each are as follows:

The Balance Scale

The Balance Scale is a universal symbol of justice. It reprents the first acadeic program of the Liceo which was the College of Law and reflects the paradigm that justice and fairness is of paramount significance interpersonal relationships within the University.

The Open Book

The Open Book symbolizes the Book of Knowledge and our uest for it as well as the teaching-learning activity and all other such related activities which are the primary concerns of the University.

The Laurel Leaves

The two branches of the Laurel Leaves stand for Victory to which every Licean must aspire to achieve in all the challenges that confront him.

The Latin motto NIL SINE NUMINE

The Latin motto NIL SINE NUMINE translate to "Nothing without Divine Will" which motto was adopted by the University's Founders subscribing to the Catholic tenet of the Faith in Divine Province.

MCMLV

The Romal Numerals MCMLV is indicative of the University Foundation Year, 1955.
